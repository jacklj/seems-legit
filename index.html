How To Play

Move through the streets, avoid roadmen, collect clues, and accuse nearby shops to expose the three money laundering fronts. You can only accuse a shop when your clue-meter is full. Wrong accusations boost enemy speed, so pick carefully.

If a roadman catches you, your clue-meter is reset to 0.If there are not enough clues left to expose the remaining bad shops, you lose.

Each round adds more shops and faster roadmen.

Controls: arrow keys (or WASD) to move, press Space to accuse a shop when you're next to it. On mobile, tap the top/bottom/left/right of the game to go up down left right. Press the accuse button below the clue-meter to accuse a shop - it'll become more prominent when you're near a shop.
