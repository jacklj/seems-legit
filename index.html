Reporting from Green Lanes

Collect clues, accuse the right fronts, and keep the heat down.

How To Play

Move through the streets, avoid roadmen, collect clues, and accuse nearby shops to expose the three money laundering fronts. You can only accuse when your clue meter is full. Wrong accusations boost enemy speed, so pick carefully. Each round adds more shops and faster roadmen.

Controls: arrow keys (or WASD) to move, press Space to accuse a shop when you're next to it.
